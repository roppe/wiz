About Us

Our History

The Reglazing Experts of Charlotte, North Carolina

Charlotte Tub Reglazing has been founded with a clear goal: to become the number one choice for homeowners and business owners who need top notch reglazing services in Charlotte, NC. We are proud to say that we have achieved our goal. We are now the best tub refinishing company in the city.


With more than 1,000 resurfacing projects completed to date, our team of reglazing experts can handle any job of any size. We can resurface bathtubs, Jacuzzis, shower stalls, sinks, wall tiles, floor tiles, and even cabinets and countertops. Get in touch with our professionals for top quality refinishing services, warranty included.

Our Goal

Choose Reglazing and Save Money

Our current goal is to save our clients as much money as possible, while also protecting the environment. Why would you replace an old bathtub, Jacuzzi or sink and go through the hassle of removing the old fixture and installing a new one?


By refinishing the fixture, you don’t need to do any construction work and don’t need to worry about waste removal. Also, a typical resurfacing job can be completed in just a couple of hours. And did you know that you can save up to 75% of the cost of replacing your fixture? Don’t send the items to the landfill; choose to refinish them!
