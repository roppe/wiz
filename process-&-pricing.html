The Reglazing Process

Getting Everything Ready

  • First, we make sure to cover the surrounding areas and to tape off everything around the fixture that will be reglazed. In case of tiles, we may need to remove the grout.
  • All cracks and chips are repaired. We will also remove any sign of rust. The surface of the item is sanded down to remove the old coating.

Applying the Primer

  • The surface will be cleaned of all dust and debris.
  • A primer is applied to the surface of the fixture. This will ensure the coating sticks to the surface.

Applying the Coating

  • Our expert carefully and uniformly applies one or two layers of coating.
  • The coating is left to cure for up to 48 hours, after which you can use the fixture.
