Our Home Remodeling Services

Here in California, home remodeling services should be just as beautiful and pristine as the scenery surrounding your home. For more than 10 years now, Green Line Home Remodeling has been helping Californians craft or restore beautiful spaces and make their dream homes come to life.

Take a look at some of the full-service home remodeling services we provide for California residents and give us a call today to see how we can improve your home now.

Bathroom

Get that modern look you’ve always wanted, find a few more square feet, or take on a full-scale bathroom remodel.

Kitchen

From new cabinets to surface restorations, new tile to rethinking the blueprint entirely, we’ll craft the kitchen of your dreams.

Interior

Any room, any surface, any need, we’ve got you covered. Let us help you design and build the perfect interior space anywhere in your home.

Exterior

We specialize in all kinds of construction works. Our certified team provides the services in time and on the highest level.

Roofing

Make your home more efficient and longer-lasting with best-in-class roof repairs and installation services in California.

Pools and Decks

Who doesn’t love a good swim? We’ll make sure your pool and pool deck are immaculate for your family and guests.

Patios

All types of stone, concrete or paver needs, we’ve got it taken care of. Let us craft or repair a gorgeous patio for you.

Paving

Need a new driveway or paving work on your existing surfaces? Call our skilled paving artisans now.

Solar Panels

Bring your home into the present and make your home more energy efficient than it has ever been with solar panels.

New Construction

Start from the ground up and let us bring your new construction vision to life. Any build, any scope, no problem.

Rooms Additions

Expand your home by building new rooms onto your space now. We can help you add any room no matter the layout of your home..

Garage Conversion

Make that garage into an extra bedroom, or vice versa! We know exactly what it takes to convert your garage perfectly.
